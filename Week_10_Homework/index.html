Week 10 Homework: Interactive Visualisations in Vega-Lite

Viz 1: Bubble Chart

This visualisation examines the relationship between the tuberculosis incidence rate and GDP of a country. The slider filter (Year) controls the time period of the data presented. The legend (Continent) is interactive; clicking on a continent will highlight all relevant countries within the bubble chart.

Viz 2: Choropleth Map

This map highlights incidence rates of various diseases around the world in the year 2021. The drop-down filter (Disease Selection) selects the disease to be presented on the map.
